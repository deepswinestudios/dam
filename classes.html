Schedule & Tuition

Tuition Policy

Tuition is due on the 1st of each month or the 1st day of class.

A late fee of $20 will be incurred after the 10th of the month. Students not current on tuition will not be permitted to take class. No exceptions.

Make-up classes are available within the month. We observe all Miami-Dade County major holidays.

For current rates, please contact us directly — we'll respond with full details as soon as possible. Rates are subject to change and every attempt is made to notify dancers in advance.

Payment Methods

We accept the following forms of payment for all studio expenses:

Cash Check Visa Mastercard Discover ATM / Debit

Credit card convenience fee: 2.5% per transaction (non-refundable).

Returned check fee: $20 service charge.
